Homebrew For Mansions of Madness: 2nd Edition

Rules

Overview

Players are divided into teams with the aim of collecting all the relics or eliminating players on the opposing teams. Players take turns to perform actions. After each player has had their turn, a Mythos phase starts and all players must resolve the required checks.

The game ends when either one team has all 4 relics in their possession or is the last team standing (have eliminated the players on the opposing team).

  • Players may use Clue tokens to convert   to   during any check.
  • If a player accumulates their total Damage, they are eliminated. They drop all relic items collected in the space they died in.
    • When a player is eliminated, they come back as a guardian monster. (Refer to Guardian monster and actions below)
  • If a player accumulates their total Horror, they become Insane and must follow the conditions on their card.
  • Individuals may also win if they complete their Insane conditions.

Player turn and actions

Each player may perform 1 Move action and 2 additional actions from the following:

  • Move: Move X (die roll) spaces.
  • Search: Search tokens for relics, items, spells, or keys. (Check required)
  • Attack: Attack an opposing player or summoned monster. (Check required)
    • Attacking can only be done if the opposing player is adjacent or within 1 space of the attacking player unless attacking with a firearm or spell (within range of 2 spaces).
    • If a player is eliminated, they must drop any relic items they have collected in the space they died. Other players may collect these items as an action.
  • Heal: Heal yourself or another ally. (Check required)
  • Trade: Trade an item with an ally.
  • Steal: Steal an item/relic from an opposing player. (Check required)
  • Place a trap: A player may place a trap to inhibit other players. Traps affect everyone and may be part of a player's Insane condition.
    • Fire: Deals 1 facedown Damage to any player that enters into a space with fire.
    • Water: Any player that enters a space with water cannot attack or use an additional move action.
    • Trapdoor: Any player that enters a space with a trapdoor must turn over 1 Damage. If they do not have any Damage, they suffer 1 facedown Damage.
  • Disarm a trap: Disarm a trap. (Check required)
    • A player may disarm a trap if they are in or adjacent to a space containing a trap.
    • A player may disarm a fire trap if they have the Fire Extinguisher item.
  • Unlock door: Unlock a door. (Key required)
    • Players are able to collect a key to use to unlock a locked door through the Search action.
  • Collect dropped relic: Collect a dropped relic from an eliminated player.

When resolving any checks, players may use Clue tokens to convert   to  .

Guardian monster and actions

If a player has been eliminated, they can spawn on the board as a Guardian monster. During their turn they may perform 3 actions from the following:

  • Move: Move X (die roll) spaces.
  • Attack: (see above)
  • Place a trap: (see above)

Guardian monsters cannot use any items, nor collect relics.

Mythos Phase

After each player has taken their turn, the Mythos phase begins in which either monsters spawn and/or attack or certain events occur. Players must resolve the required checks. Once all players have resolved their checks, players can take their turns and the cycle repeats.
